Our Philosophy

Reading is a skill necessary for all aspects of life. Without the ability to read a person is unable to live a successful life in society. Teaching children to read is one of, if not the most important things a teacher can do. Before any teacher can begin to do their job there needs to be an understanding of the students. This is especially true of reading which is impacted so heavily by a student’s home life and background. There are many different approaches to teaching reading and each of them has their supporters and their detractors. My personal view, on teaching reading, is that, in order for children to learn as much as they possibly can, reading instruction needs to use a variety of models and not just one model all of the time.
